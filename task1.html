About One Piece

The journey of freedom and dreams

One Piece is a legendary anime and manga created by Eiichiro Oda. It tells the story of Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate with the power of the Gum-Gum Devil Fruit, whose dream is to become the Pirate King.
